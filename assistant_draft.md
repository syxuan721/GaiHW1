# About Me and My Interests

My name is Yaxuan Shi and I'm a graduate student in Information Systems. I've always been curious about how technology shapes the way we work and make decisions, which is what drew me to this field. Lately, most of my focus has been on artificial intelligence and how it's starting to show up in everyday tools and systems.

One area I'm especially interested in is generative AI — the kind of AI that can write text, generate images, or produce code based on a prompt. It feels like we're at an early but important moment with this technology, and I want to better understand both what it can do and where it falls short.

A few ways generative AI can be useful in this space:

- **Content drafting** — quickly producing first drafts of reports, emails, or documentation that humans can then review and refine
- **Code assistance** — helping developers write, debug, and explain code, which can speed up development and lower the barrier for beginners
- **Data summarization** — turning large amounts of text or structured data into clear, readable summaries for faster decision-making
